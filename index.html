About Me

I am a passionate Master's student in Artificial Intelligence & Business Analytics at the University of South Florida, dedicated to transforming data into actionable business solutions.

My expertise spans across data analytics, machine learning, cloud computing, and data visualization. I thrive at the intersection of technical problem-solving and strategic decision-making ,whether it's building predictive models, designing cloud-based solutions, or creating dashboards that tell compelling stories.

My key interests include predictive analytics, natural language processing, and business intelligence strategy. Through my projects, I've tackled diverse challenges from airline delay optimization and healthcare data visualization to loan risk prediction and sentiment analysis.

Beyond academics, I actively explore emerging AI tools, mentor peers, and contribute to open-source projects. My mission is to bridge the gap between data science, business strategy, and innovation to drive meaningful impact.
