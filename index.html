About Me

I am a Master's student in Artificial Intelligence & Business Analytics at the University of South Florida. I am passionate about using data to solve business problems, optimize operations, and uncover actionable insights.

My expertise spans across data analytics, machine learning, cloud computing, and data visualization. I enjoy combining technical problem-solving with strategic decision-making — whether it’s building predictive models, designing cloud-based solutions, or creating dashboards that tell a story.

Key interests include predictive analytics, natural language processing, and business intelligence strategy. My projects range from airline delay optimization and healthcare data visualization to loan risk prediction and sentiment analysis.

Beyond academics, I enjoy exploring emerging AI tools, mentoring peers, and contributing to open-source projects. My goal is to bridge the gap between data science, business strategy, and innovation.
